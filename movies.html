A List of Recent Movies Dustin Enjoyed the Most

My Taste

My taste in movies spans a pretty wide variety, but I'd have to say over-all I enjoy lighter-weight, positive movies that leave me feeling, well, happy. I find I tend to watch movies with a decent amount of comedy in them, but also a good action movie with a good plot captivates me. If I can get an action movie with humor incorporated into it, then it takes the cake!

Is There a Number One?

Out of all the movies I saw over the past year, there are 2 that compete in my mind for the best movie I saw last year. I saw Thor earlier in the year and is easily the best Marvel movie to date. It took the humor of marvel movies to the max, and also felt similar to an Avengers movie as it had some connections to other Marvel characters. Star Wars on the other hand took a little bit of a more serious approach, but Star Wars has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I love the nostalgia of watching any Star Wars movie, but I love seeing it with modern technology being used to make it look just gorgeous! I also found this movie made me love the character Kylo Ren so much more. He seems like he has a much deeper character now. So, is there a number one? I have to say no, there are two number ones.

All the movies I'll be talking about are movies I've seen in the last year. 2017 saw some pretty awesome releases! As for my Top 5 Favorites they are as follows, in no particular order:

  • Star Wars: The Last Jedi
  • Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle
  • Thor: Ragnarok
  • Guardian of the Galaxy: Vol. 2
  • Spiderman: Homecoming
