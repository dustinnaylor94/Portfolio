I Love Saabs

My Saab Story

I love Saabs! But I haven't always loved these vehicles as much as I do now. Infact, I didn't even know what a Saab was until 2015! When I returned from serving an LDS mission, I was gifted a non-working 1990 Hyundai Excel. THAT THING WAS GUTLESS! But it got me around. After a while I started to get sick of this little old car with over 250k miles on it. One day I got together with my buddy, Justin, and he had bought an interesting new car: a Saab 900 SPG in Edwarian Grey. It was so unique, and quite comfy!

Eventually I found myself searching KSL for cars and remembered Justin's Saab. I did some searches and asked my friend some questions and eventually happened upon a 1990 Saab 900 SPG in Talledega Red for only $2000 dollars. I took one test drive of this beautiful little turbocharged car and instantly fell in love. Today, it is still my daily driver, 2 and a half years later.

Eventually I got my wife to switch from a Toyota Corolla into a gorgeous 2006 Saab 9-3 Sportcombi Aero. This car has a whopping 320 hoarsepower as compared to the Corolla's mighty 130. She loves it! To this day, I love Saabs and hope to be able to hang on to my 900 for the rest of my life and restore it to it's total potential.

Saabs are a relatively recent love of mine.

Thank You for Visiting! -Dustin Naylor
